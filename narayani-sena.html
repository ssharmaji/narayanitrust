Narayani Sena

Narayani Sena:

Shri Sankat Mochan Balaji Temple Management Committee was set up by Manish Yadav in 2005 with the main objective of renovation of Shri Hanumanji Temple Kataiyapur and in memory of his elder brother Late Dalveer Singh Yadav. The said committee first renovated the temple of Shri Hanuman ji and then built the Vaishnavi temple in the year 2007, in which the idol of Mata Durga ji, along with mother Laxmi Devi, mother Saraswati Devi and Sheetla Devi and Rama in the court made Rama Laxman and Mata Sita ji | After that, Lord Sri Krishna and Radha ji temple were built in 2014, after that the park, garden saint residence, small gaushala in the temple were constructed in the temple premises in which every year old manva mela fair, Navratri fair Shivratri, Shri Krishna Janmashtami, Kudarkot Various religious functions like Sadhu Seva, Guru Purnima Festival in Parikrama, the wedding ceremony of poor girls every year are organized and the programs are conducted by themselves. Gaushala was also run by Shri Sankat Mochan Balaji Temple Management Committee in which around 1400 cows are fed. Various social and religious work is done by Manish Yadav. His life has been a struggle and a challenge from the very beginning, Manish Yadav continues to inspire the youth for social and religious work due to being an environmental lover and beloved of cow service and religion. He contested from Jaswantnagar Assembly on Bharatiya Janata Party ticket in 2017.He also formed Narayani Trust for the establishment of religion by which the Narayani Army was formed in the year 2020 to create awareness and mobilize the youth of the society for social and religious work.
