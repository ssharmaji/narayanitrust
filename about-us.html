About Narayani Trust

This 'Narayani Trust' is the great saint of Sri Vaishnavism, Sri Sri 1008 Jagannathji Maharaj Sthan-Aste Kutiya, Achalda and Sri Sri 1008 Shri Gurujanadji Ji Maharaj alias Andhe Baba Sthan-Bahadurpur Luhiya, Basrehar, Etawah saints and Ashram tradition of temples. The Narayani Trust has been formed by social workers Shri Manish Yadav "Patre" and saints to keep alive the life of Jirnodwar, Rashtra Seva, Goseva, Sant Seva, regular worship, religious rituals, social service, Guru Shishya tradition. The entire arrangement of temples run by the trust is being done by the respective sub-committees. In this sequence, Shri Sankat Mochan Balaji Temple Management Committee (Reg.) Is running Shri Hanuman Ji Temple, Nagla Mardan, Kataipur Ashram and Parauli Ramayan Gaushala and other temples and gaushalas.

Two sub-organizations have also been formed for the purpose of the Narayani Trust. Sant Samaj Suraksha Parishad was formed for the protection of saint society, sacrifice and propagation of religion. It will be conducted by saints only.

The youth have been constituted as a non-political and religious and social organization by gathering organizational power towards religion, nation, society and their rights, duties, which will be called "Narayani Sena Uttar Pradesh". Which will be governed by Mr. Manish Yadav "Patre", President of the Trust, which will be set up at the state, division, district, development block, Nyaya Panchayat and Gram Panchayat levels.
